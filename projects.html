PearlHacks 2018 Alexa Skill


My team won the award for Best Use of Amazon Web Services in the 2018 PearlHacks hackathon at UNC Chapel Hill. Our project was an Alexa skill that says a quote from a famous women leader in business and technology, as part of the hackathon's goal to support females in technology. We utilized AWS Lambda services, coding in Node.js, to host and test the skill, as well as AWS's Alexa Skill tools. The skill was a multi-level skill, in which someone says: "Alexa, empower me." Alexa responds, "Would you like to be empowered?". The user responds, "Yes." Alexa responds by reading out one of around 10 quotes from famous women leaders in technology.

Skills: JavaScript, Amazon Web Services, Product Management
